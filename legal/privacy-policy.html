Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Global Market School Ltd. ("we," "our," "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding it.

When you enroll in a course, we collect:

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. Information is shared only with the service providers listed above, strictly as needed to deliver our services.

We take reasonable technical measures to protect your information, including secure, encrypted payment processing and restricted access to stored data. No system can be guaranteed 100% secure, but we are committed to protecting your information appropriately.

6. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting us at info@globalmarketschool.com.
